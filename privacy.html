Privacy Policy

Black Soap Games operates mobile games published on the Google Play Store. This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information Collection and Use

Our games may collect certain information automatically for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, and in-app purchases.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These include:

  • Unity Diagnostics & Analytics
  • Google Analytics
  • Google AdMob
  • Unity In-App Purchases

These services may collect information such as device identifiers, IP address, usage data, crash logs, and ad interaction data.

Cookies

Our games do not directly use cookies, but third-party SDKs may use similar technologies to collect information and improve their services.

Children’s Information

Black Soap Games does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
